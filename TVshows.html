The best TV shows I have ever seen

Top 3

Anne with An E(2017)

It is about Anne Shirley, an orphan who challenges societal norms, fights for equality, and finds her place in Avonlea. The series highlights themes of identity, resilience, and gender empowerment.

Shameless(2011)

Fiona Gallagher, a tough, resourceful young woman balancing wild ambition and family duty as she fights to hold her reckless siblings together while chasing her own dreams in a world that never plays fair.

Vampire Diaries(2009)

Thrilling supernatural drama where Elena Gilbert is torn between two vampire brothers, Stefan and Damon, in the mysterious town of Mystic Falls. Love, danger, and dark secrets collide in this addictive tale of fate and immortality.
